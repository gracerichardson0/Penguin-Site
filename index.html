Welcome to the Burke Penguin Reserve!

Here, we take care of penguins of multiple species, including Adelie Penguins, Emperor Penguins, Chinstrap Penguins, and more! You can browse our website to find out information on the types of penguins that we look over, as well as the history behind penguins and their evolution. You can also find a way to visit our penguin habitat and can meet penguins face to face, after filling out the application with your personal details. We look forward to seeing you soon!



Our mission at the Burke Penguin Reserve:


  • We take care of penguins, including endangered species.
  • We feed, protect, and ensure the future of dying penguins.
  • We rehabilitate and do veterinary work on injured penguins.
